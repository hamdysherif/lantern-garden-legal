# Privacy Policy — Lantern Garden

**Effective date:** `2026-07-04`
**App:** Lantern Garden (iOS & Android)
**Developer / Data controller:** `Hamdy Mokhtar Sherif` ("we", "us")
**Contact:** `lanterngarden.app@gmail.com`

## 1. Summary

Lantern Garden is a cozy tap-to-rotate puzzle game. The game is playable offline,
and we do **not** require you to create an account or give us your name, email, or
other contact details. We rely on a small set of well-known third-party services
for crash reporting, anonymous analytics, optional advertising, and in-app
purchases. This policy explains what those services process and the choices you
have. **We do not sell your personal information.**

## 2. Information we and our partners process

We do not directly collect your real-world identity. Our third-party SDKs may
process the following, typically tied to a device or advertising identifier:

| Data type | Purpose | Processor |
|---|---|---|
| App usage & in-game events (levels played, sessions, taps) | Improve difficulty and game design (aggregate/anonymous analytics) | Google Firebase Analytics |
| Crash logs & diagnostics (device model, OS version, stack traces) | Diagnose crashes and improve stability | Google Firebase Crashlytics |
| Remote-configuration fetch (install/device data) | Tune difficulty and ad limits without an app update | Google Firebase Remote Config |
| Advertising identifier (IDFA/AAID) & ad-interaction data | Serve and measure ads (personalized only with your consent) | Google AdMob |
| Purchase & entitlement records, anonymous app-user ID | Process, restore, and manage in-app purchases | RevenueCat + Apple App Store / Google Play billing |
| Player ID, leaderboard & achievement data (only if you use them) | Game Center / Google Play Games features | Apple Game Center / Google Play Games |
| Game progress and settings | Save your game **on your device only** — not transmitted to us | Local storage (on-device) |

We do **not** collect precise location, contacts, photos, microphone, or camera
data. Game progress and settings are stored locally on your device.

## 3. How we use the information

- Operate, maintain, and improve the game and its difficulty balance.
- Diagnose and fix crashes and technical problems.
- Provide, restore, and manage in-app purchases.
- Serve advertising (see §4) and measure its performance.
- Offer optional leaderboards/achievements if you choose to use them.

## 4. Advertising & consent

- The app shows ads via **Google AdMob**. Ads help keep the game free; a one-time
  **"Remove Ads"** purchase disables them.
- **Personalized ads require your consent.** In the EEA/UK we present the **Google
  UMP (User Messaging Platform)** consent prompt; on iOS we present Apple's **App
  Tracking Transparency (ATT)** prompt before using the advertising identifier for
  tracking. If you decline, you still see ads, but non-personalized ones.
- The app is set to a **non-child-directed (13+)** audience, and ads are limited to
  a maximum content rating suitable for that audience.

## 5. Legal bases (GDPR/EEA/UK users)

- **Consent** — advertising and non-essential analytics (via UMP / ATT).
- **Legitimate interests** — crash reporting and core functionality/security.
- **Contract** — processing in-app purchases you choose to make.

## 6. Children

Lantern Garden is family-friendly but is **not directed to children under 13**
(or the equivalent minimum age in your region). We do not knowingly collect
personal data from children. If you believe a child has provided us data, contact
us at `lanterngarden.app@gmail.com` and we will delete it.

## 7. Your choices & rights

- **Ads / tracking:** decline via the in-app consent prompt (EEA), the iOS ATT
  prompt (Settings → Privacy & Security → Tracking), or reset/limit your
  advertising ID in device settings. Buy "Remove Ads" to disable ads entirely.
- **Access, correction, deletion, portability (GDPR):** email
  `lanterngarden.app@gmail.com`; we will action requests and forward them to our
  processors where applicable.
- **California (CCPA/CPRA):** you have the right to know, delete, and correct, and
  to opt out of "sale"/"sharing." We do **not** sell personal information. You will
  not be discriminated against for exercising these rights.
- **Revoke consent:** change your device ad/tracking settings at any time.

## 8. Data retention

We retain data only as long as needed for the purposes above. Diagnostic and
analytics data follow the processors' defaults (e.g. Firebase Crashlytics ≈ 90
days; Analytics per project settings). Purchase records are kept as required for
entitlement restoration and tax/accounting.

## 9. How to request deletion

Email `lanterngarden.app@gmail.com` from your request. You can also delete all
locally-stored game data by uninstalling the app. For store-held purchase data,
use your Apple/Google account controls.

## 10. Security

We use reputable providers that transmit data over encrypted connections (HTTPS/
TLS). No method of transmission or storage is 100% secure, but we take reasonable
measures to protect the limited data processed.

## 11. International transfers

Our processors (Google, Apple, RevenueCat) may process data in countries other
than yours, including the United States, under safeguards such as Standard
Contractual Clauses where required.
